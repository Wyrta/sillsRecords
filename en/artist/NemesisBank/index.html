Nemesis Bank

Funded at Angers in 2018, Nemesis Bank is a french rock band created by guitarist Adel Raimbeaud and drummer Pierre Marais. Nemesis Bank made its first release in 2020 with the electro EP Hybrid POS.In 2021 came out their 2nd EP Wildly Underwelming with wider music aspects and unprecedented featurings. Working on its first album Presque Metal, Nemesis Bank is ready to dive.
